About TCET
Thakur College of Engineering & Technology (TCET) was established in the Academic year 2001-02 with a clear objective of providing quality technical education in tune with international standards and contemporary global requirements. The Institute is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) & Government of Maharashtra and is affiliated to the University of Mumbai (UOM). It offers six Undergraduate, three Postgraduate and a Doctoral - Ph.D.(Technology) programme. TCET is an Un-aided hindi linguistic minory Institute.
In the year 2011, Computer Engineering (CMPN), Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (EXTC) and Information Technology (IT) courses got accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for 3 years w.e.f. 16.9.2011. The NBA expert team visited for Re-accreditation of CMPN, EXTC, IT and First time Accreditation of Electronics Engineering is completed during 12-14 Feb. 2016.Results are declared on NBA website on 27/04/2016 and accredited for 3 Years w.e.f. ist July 2016 as per revised guidelines.  Read more...
