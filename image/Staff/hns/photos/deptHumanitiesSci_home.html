Vision
“The department of Engineering Sciences and Humanities shall strive to provide powerful educational effectiveness by linking facts, theory, inquiry, discovery and solutions to real world problems thereby providing a sound foundation to the undergraduate students.”
Mission
An endeavor to provide a strong base in engineering and technology, where students, faculty and staff work collaboratively to expand knowledge in the basic disciplines that is appropriate to their career goals, equipping well with knowledge and skills that will allow them to function as responsible and contributing members of society.
Program Specific Outcomes
PSO1:  
Evolve as technocrats and entrepreneurs in emerging fields and committed towards social and environmental issues. Foster essential research and innovative thinking to find optimal solutions to complex problems to meet socio-economic needs.
PSO2:  
Professional development through practical, training, project, certification, and innovative engineering solutions using research and problem-solving skills. Utilize proficiency skills in advanced technology, and expertise in design and development.
PSO3:  
Apply multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary approaches to design, analyse, synthesise and validate real-time problems.
PSO4:  
Excellence in communication skills and development of leadership skills, ethical values, and environmental consciousness as well as development of personal and professional approaches through lifelong learning.
